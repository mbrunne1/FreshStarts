How It Works

Many career changers struggle because they describe their experience in industry-specific language that doesn't translate. Our tool helps you:

  1. Identify your transferable skills - What you do that applies anywhere
  2. Reframe your experience - Translate industry jargon into universal business language
  3. Find unexpected opportunities - Discover careers you didn't know you were qualified for
