Age-Friendly Job Board

Opportunities from employers who value experience and actively hire career changers over 30. Stop wasting time on companies that won't appreciate what you bring.

Why This Job Board Is Different

Most job boards are optimized for recent graduates or people with linear career paths. FreshStart features companies that:

  • Value transferable skills over exact job titles
  • Appreciate life experience and maturity
  • Offer career-changer friendly training and onboarding
  • Don't discriminate by age (explicitly or implicitly)
  • Understand non-linear career paths aren't red flags
